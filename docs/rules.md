# Game Rules

## Album Rules
- Albums cannot repeat within the same game

## Review Rules
- Users cannot review their own album
- One review per user per round
- Ratings: decimal between 1.0–10.0

## Scoring
- Final score = average of submitted ratings
- Missing reviews are excluded

## Visibility
- Reviews hidden until round completion

## Round Completion
- All reviews submitted OR host advances

## Constraints
- Max 6 users per group
